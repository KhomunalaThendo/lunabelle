About Us

Welcome to LunaBelle, your number one source for all things beauty. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of beauty products, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2023 by Thend, LunaBelle has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When Thend first started out, his passion for eco-friendly beauty products drove him to quit his day job and do tons of research, so LunaBelle can offer you the world's most advanced beauty products. We now serve customers all over the US and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
